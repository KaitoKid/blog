Warriors Take It To Game Seven

Posted on 2018-05-26

GAME SEVEN

Annnnnd here we go. This is definitely the real finals of this NBA season, whatever happens next can’t even come close to the Warriors vs Rockets. No matter if Celtics take the East or the Cavs, they won’t stand a chance against the West. Since it does fall on Memorial Day, I’m hoping to head out to a local bar around Oakland or Berkeley, to really get that playoffs vibe. I’m pretty sure wherever we go around Pleasanton, it’s not going to be as lit as up in the East Bay.

Other News

I don’t really have much other news. I went home this weekend so I could clean out my apartment in Berkeley in preparation for my subletters this summer, I can’t believe that I have two different people subletting. The first pair is a couple and they’re bringing their own beds, so I had to deconstruct mine and move it out into the living room. Thank’s Denny for letting me use your space. The landlord is gunna replace our fire alarms so they don’t go off in a chain reaction anymore, and removing the old couch that nobody uses.

I brought my Alesis V25 back to Pleasanton so hopefully I can make that work out. In terms of better news, I did just pick up a new skateboard! I’ve always wanted to skateboard so I thought now is as good of a time as any.

Welcome Gin

I named her Gin because it kinda sounds cool like a name from Gintama or Detective Conan… also because it’s called Gin and Tonic by Landyachtz. Apparently the Dinghy is supposed to be fantastic for commuting. I’m deathly scared of getting injured so I did pick up a helmet, elbow and knee pads, and a wrist brace. Go me! I also just decided on the spot that she’s a female.

Tattoos Galore

Ok, I also did just get some InkBox tattoos in the mail. As soon as I finish writing this post, I’m gunna go put them on. They seem absolutely ridiculously cool, I feel like I’m getting my rebellious phase 10 years late LOL. I picked two geeky ones, one is the hearts from Zelda, and another is the Ouroboros tattoo from Full Metal Alchemist.

Tired

Oh yea, not sure why but I’ve been feeling suuuuuper tired again lately, like no matter how much I sleep, I’m still tired. It really is affecting my ability to focus as well as my balance or something. It’s really trippy but it’s basically like how I felt in the past. Maybe it comes in waves periodically? Like the vestibular migraines the doctor talked about? Anyways, it’s really shitty (oh yea, it could be insomnia too, hopefully working out helps) so I’m praying for the best.

Bouldering

Can’t wait to start going to the gym again! I’m making this summer the most transformative yet for me. Tomorrow I’m hitting up the bouldering gym for the first time in three years with Jinni and her roommate, other interns at Workday. My excuses in the past were that I couldn’t find my shoes, or that I can’t figure out how to get to the climbing gym. Well, now I don’t have either but I also am genuinely interested in climbing again.

Can’t think of much else to say, so I’m calling it for this post. Shoot, gotta do my homework for my online classes too ugh.

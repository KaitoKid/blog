Second and Third Days

Posted on 2018-05-23

Streak Broken

I can’t believe I broke the streak already. To be honest, I was literally thinking about blogging yesterday before I even left work, but by the time I got home, I just got so depressed by the Warriors loss to the Rockets that I went straight to sleep.

During day 2 of orientation, we learned more about the intern roadmap, meeting up with our managers, and what to expect from this internship. Throughout the introductions, there were definitely quite a few go bears thrown in here and there. I think almost 1/10th of the entire intern workforce is from Berkeley.

I also swapped out my Macbook Air for a Macbook Pro, but it was quite a painful process because I had to leave orientation. My first trip I accidentally took Noah’s backpack, and by the time I made it back, the activity was over. Oops.

Day Three

Today was an interesting day. I had to miss out on the XpressO (WorkDay’s proprietary language) training since I had to go grab my passport from back home in Berkeley. Along the way I got my rash checked out, and turns out I have a jock itch/ringworm. The cream for this literally burns like hell oh my god I hate it so much. I didn’t bring my WorkDay backpack with me, so after getting my I-9/employment eligibility worked out, I just went straight home - doing the bootcamp remotely.

Unforunately for Gayashan, his team got absolutely destroyed in the finals for the East in the Stanley Cup. Poor guy, since apparently Tampa had the number one offense in the league - until game 5. They haven’t been able to score a single point since then. He followed up with making deviled eggs for his team’s picnic tomorrow. I’m actually really surprised by how easy it is to make, as well as how well he can cook. Hopefully I can pick up a few things from him this summer. He’s honestly such a cool guy even though we have a 7 year difference between us. I really do feel like I lucked out, can’t wait to spend the rest of these 11 weeks with him.

Gym Situation

Ok now this is a real problem. WorkDay has a gym for $25 per month that I intend to go to, but I also really do want to pick up climbing again. Jinni expressed that she wants to go (and she’s hoping to coerce her roommate into climbing so we can get a ride to The Peak in Fremont!) I’m really hoping my jock itch clears up somewhat by then… otherwise it’ll be quite a painful experience.

Closing

Well, that’s it for today. I still need to get a power brick from Samsung, get an ethernet cable for my PC, fix the wifi issue so Gayashan can use wifi in his room, setup a dentist and vision appointment… oh man there’s a lot. Signing out

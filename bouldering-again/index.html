Bouldering Again

Posted on 2018-05-28

The Peak of Fremont

Well, I can officially say that I’m back to bouldering again. I registered with Jinni, another fellow Workday intern (and I finally realized that Workday is spelled without a capital D) cus it was the closest location, and it has a summer $150 special for 3 months. This will last us all the way until the end of our internships. Additionally, we may be getting $50/mo from Workday for gym memberships and other health related materials/activities so hopefully that pulls through.

My arms are sore. Like stupidly sore. I haven’t seriously exercised since the last Under Armour x Cal event where Chelsey and I did a HIIT class. I couldn’t even make it half the way and I was sore for days afterwards. I’m not looking forward to waking up tomorrow - I can barely even like squeeze out my toothpaste LOL. Stretching my forearms as much as I can.

Rhea was our driver today, and she did it out the generosity of her heart. She was originally going to climb but then chose not to, so she shopped around nearby and took us home. What. A. Mom. Not only that, but Rhea was also the roommate of Revati last year. What are the chances that we’d be connected through Jinni and Revati. Small world. Hopefully she’ll be able to join in next time! I’m planning to go climbing at least 2 times a week, possibly three times if the stars align. Otherwise I’ll probably show up to work early, hit the gym after, and take a Scoop home.

InkBox

Surprisingly, InkBox pulled through, as in, it works a lot better than I thought it would. I’ll attach a photo here eventually but for now, I can’t figure out how to get images to work properly on Hexo. I placed it on my wrist, and it got noticed today woohoo.

This wasn’t super clean, so I’m hoping my next attempt will be better. I haven’t decided where I’d put the next yet because it’s quite big and not as subtle. (The ouroboros one)

Boujie Apartment

I nearly forgot, but damn this apartment is boujie. Once again, I promise I’ll stick in photos as soon as I figure it out. I’m planning to host a party with OCTO and other friends here.

GAMING

YES. I GOT MY ETHERNET IN THE MAIL TODAY. It’s wired up super ratchet but you know what, it works. I got in my first games of Fortnite in ages. Well, I say games but I actually played with my old friends Merumi, etc. (from my Soccer Spirits days, but that’s a story for another time) for almost 5 hours.

Yep.

Didn’t mean for that to happen (and I had to eat canned soup with rice AGAIN for dinner) but I’m glad it did, had so much fun which I’ve been missing out on. We still gotta fix the wifi issue though cus poor Gayashan doesn’t get any signal in his room.

Work Work Work

Yea, I got a lot of work to catch up on. I feel like my anxiety and my insomnia really do stem from falling behind on work and not being able to catch up. I really intend to get in a good amount of work done tomorrow, and hopefully I’ll be able to follow up with these priorities:

  1. Homework for my CogSci class
  2. DeepScribe
  3. Workday (XpressO lessons)

Worst part is, it’s the Warriors vs Rockets game 7 tomorrow which I HAVE to watch. I told Gayashan I’d hit up a bar with him in Oakland or Berkeley but I don’t think I’ll be able to follow through with that - but to be fair, I don’t think he’ll mind. He isn’t exactly a fan of either team anyways. However, whichever team wins will basically win the conference finals.

The Cavs put up one hell of a fight, and so did the Celtics, but they’ll both fall to the West. Just watch.

Closing

In closing, I just have a few other things on my mind. I’m pretty sure nobody reads this so I don’t really care - so I was told some tips from my doctor on how to desensitize myself, so hopefully that works out. Also, there’s still so much I need to do this week, I really need some time off where I take a whole day to myself where I’m not trying to meet people, impress people, and so that I can just be the potato I am.

Signing out (:
